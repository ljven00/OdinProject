Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 garlic clove, unpeeled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 250g pasta shells
  • 1/2 small pack parsley
  • 1/2 small pack basil
  • 30g toasted pine nuts
  • 1/2 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 30g parmesan

Preparation

  1. Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6.
  2. Toss the broccoli and garlic in 1 tbsp of the olive oil on a roasting tray and roast in the oven for 10-12 mins, until softened.
  3. Tip the pasta shells into a pan of boiling, salted water.
  4. Cook according to packet instructions and drain.
  5. Tip the parsley, basil, pine nuts, lemon juice and parmesan into a blender.
  6. Once the broccoli is done, set aside a few of the smaller pieces.
  7. Squeeze the garlic from its skin, add to the blender along with the rest of the broccoli, pulse to a pesto and season well.
  8. Toss the pasta with the pesto.
  9. Add the reserved broccoli florets, split between two bowls and top with a little extra parmesan, the lemon zest and a good grinding of black pepper.
